About

Brothergood consists of two Finnish brothers, Timo (lyrics, rapping, vocals) and Juha (composer, producer, vocals).
They are based in Lieto and Turku, respectively.

  • 1980's

    Early childhood

    The brothers have a long musical history dating back to 1980’s when Juha had his first keyboards and Timo had his drums. Some songs were recorded already back then, using a boombox-like cassette recorder. Luckily, that evidence seems to be completely lost today.

  • Early 2000's

    First semi-professional songs done

    The first hiphop/rap songs were made together in the early 2000’s. While Juha had his interest in electronic dance music, Timo had been influenced by rap-artists like Talib Kweli and Kanye West and started to work on his own lyrical ideas. At that time, Timo wrote music mostly about his own life while Juha stayed more in the background forces. During the years, their style has varied a lot. Luckily, most of the songs have been buried in undisclosed locations.

  • 2013

    Brothergood is born

    The final concept of making music together was established in 2013. The brothers both work on song ideas, collaborating partly also on the lyrics and the melodies. Brothergood was born.

  • November 2013

    First song released

    The first released song was Goodbye June, which was selected as a “weekly garage band song” on a Finnish major radio station YleX. The song received encouraging feedback from people liking all kinds of musical genres. The Brothergood style was found. Brothergood stands for everything positive, everything good.

    January 2015

    First album release

    After 1,5 years of work, Brothergood releases their first EP album called Beautiful. Album includes five songs and is available in major digital music stores and streaming services.
